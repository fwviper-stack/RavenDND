[Ring of Unfinished Grace]
Ring, cursed, divine binding, requires attunement

Description
A slender silver ring worked so finely it almost seems grown instead of forged. Once placed upon the finger, it settles as though it had always belonged there.

Known Effect
While wearing and attuned to the ring, the wearer’s body repairs itself with unnatural steadiness.
- regain 1 hit point every 10 minutes while at 1 hit point or more
- minor cuts, bruises, and ordinary bodily wear fade with unusual speed
- the body resists simple decline, rot, and natural wasting better than it should
Hidden Divine Curse
The ring bears a judgment laid by Corellon Larethian.
Its healing is inseparable from its refusal to let the body pass naturally through time.
Once the ring fully binds:
- the wearer’s body ceases natural aging
- the wearer cannot mature physically beyond the life stage at which the ring bound itself
- the wearer cannot be aged by ordinary magical means while the binding holds
- the wearer’s mind may continue to learn, remember, and emotionally develop, but the body remains unfinished
Binding Rule
Once placed on the finger and bound, the ring cannot be removed by ordinary mortal means.
Common curse-breaking magic is not sufficient to end the full divine judgment.
Release Rule
The judgment may end if:
1. Corellon Larethian willingly releases it
2. the wearer fulfills an accepted atonement
3. the divine will behind it goes unrenewed (for 100 years - Elven adulthood)
