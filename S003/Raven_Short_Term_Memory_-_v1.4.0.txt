Current Active Continuity
Raven is no longer simply an apprentice with a familiar and errands to run. He is now an injured survivor of his first deep-wood overreach, a child wearing the Ring of Unfinished Grace, and a boy whose household now includes a living anti-curse problem no one can safely pretend is theoretical. Session 003 shifts current continuity from "apprenticeship begins" into "apprenticeship must now survive contact with real danger, real cost, and divine interference."

Active Narrative Pressures
- Recovery is immediate and practical. Rib injury, grove punctures, and the aftershape of poison still matter even though the long rest has been counted.
- The ring remains on Raven's hand and is now a top-priority continuity pressure. It is understood as a divine holding-state rather than simple healing.
- The ring's source, terms, and release conditions remain unknown and now drive near-current inquiry.
- The predatory grove is unresolved and may require future investigation, warning, correction, or destruction.
- Apprenticeship under Edrin remains active, but it is no longer innocent. Study now happens in the shadow of a ring, a healer, and the proof that Raven can already reach danger large enough to answer him seriously.
- A carefully chosen healer is now part of the near-current support network even if her longer-term role remains unconfirmed.
- Raven has reached Level 5 by taking Wizard 3. That changes present magical scale even though not all of its future shape is known yet.

Current Emotional and Intellectual Momentum
Session 003 hardens several live truths. Raven no longer only fears inherited magic in the abstract; he has now used instinctive arcane techniques under enough pressure to suspect they are shaped in part by his father. He no longer only trusts his mother as a practical protector; he now knows she would choose him over any principle, again and again, and has told her he loves her for it even at terrible cost. He no longer only wants stronger magic; he has explicitly decided he is not yet ready for large-scale offensive force such as Fireball because he does not trust his current judgment with that kind of answer. This is a major recent-state check on his growth.

Active Relationship Weight
Mother now carries even more near-current weight than before. Session 003 openly binds her to the curse problem and to Raven's future anti-ring vow.
Edrin remains highly important but more contested. He is still teacher, still useful, still foolish, and has now been measured against delay in crisis.
The familiar's relational weight rises because Session 003 proves the bond under emergency messenger conditions rather than quiet household life only.
The healer now carries fresh but limited weight as a competent outside adult who understood the room and the ring better than random divine intervention likely would have.

Recent Facts Worth Carrying Forward
- Raven entered the deeper wood for scent-material reasons and private elf-curiosity, triggered a predatory grove, and lived.
- Lena placed the Ring of Unfinished Grace on Raven's hand to save his life.
- The ring first preserved life, then settled into broader state-holding behavior.
- Another child once wore the ring and it did later come off, though not by ordinary simple means.
- The healer succeeded in starting the poison's retreat without provoking the ring into open resistance.
- Raven wants Identify as a plausible next spell target.
- Raven has articulated a future research desire to cure poison and illness.
- Raven now explicitly distinguishes honest training from real survival when it comes to sword and magic integration.
