Session Opening Position

Session 002 opens at the exact pressure-point where Session 001 stopped: Raven has forced honesty out of Edrin around Find Familiar, but the spell is not yet fully learned or cast. He begins the session under his mother's eye, with sharpened frustration, rising confidence, and the practical certainty that if Edrin cannot actually teach him, he may one day need another road entirely. The day begins in domestic routine, but it is already carrying more weight than routine should. Raven wants a real lesson, not another threshold.

Morning with Mother — Judgment, Wandering Ambition, and the Shape of a Wider World

The session first deepens Raven's relationship with his mother through a long morning of sparring, breakfast, and plain spoken thinking. He tells her that if he cannot truly learn from Edrin, he may someday need another teacher. She refuses both childish bluffing and passive surrender, forcing him to distinguish dependence from helplessness. In the same conversation Raven speaks openly of Waterdeep, travel, and the restlessness of outgrowing the small size of his current world. His mother does not mock that hunger. She reframes it as preparation rather than impulse: skill, coin, judgment, and reasons for arriving somewhere larger. Session 002 therefore clarifies that Raven's desire for a wider world is no longer vague fantasy. It is becoming a practical future pressure.

Edrin's House and the End of Delay

For the first time Raven enters Edrin's home rather than meeting only in the yard. That physical crossing matters. The room itself confirms what Raven already suspected: Edrin had prepared for the confrontation before admitting it to himself. Raven presses him directly, first through humor and then through refusal to leave without the lesson answered honestly. Edrin finally stops hiding the issue behind caution. He admits that he delayed not because Raven lacked readiness, but because he himself feared becoming the man who actually taught the spell. That admission changes the entire teacher-student dynamic. The lesson ceases to be managed delay and becomes real instruction.

The Full Structure of Find Familiar

The heart of Session 002 is not merely that Raven learns Find Familiar, but that he is taught the spell in a form worthy of trust. Together Raven and Edrin shape the four anchor structure of the working: Purpose, Invitation, Boundary, and Welcome. Raven insists on language that fits his mouth rather than borrowed grandeur, and Edrin increasingly respects him for that. The final structure fixes the bond around partnership, mutual understanding, exclusion of coercion and taint, and welcome beside Raven rather than beneath him. The lesson then deepens into materials, sequence of working, focus discipline, and failure states. Edrin teaches not only how to call, but how to avoid lying to oneself inside the call. Session 002 therefore transforms Find Familiar from an admired spell into a governed structure Raven can actually carry.

First Honest Casting and the Arrival of the Familiar

The most important event of the session is Raven's first real casting of Find Familiar. Before the live attempt he names a fear that reaches all the way back to his father: the fear of being shaped by magic instead of shaping it rightly. Edrin accepts that fear as legitimate and helps frame it as respect for possibility rather than surrender to outcome. Under that honesty, Raven completes the working. He does not force it, does not flatter himself into false recognition, and does not mistake intensity for answer. The response arrives as a small dark cat-shaped spirit whose presence feels stable, watchful, and right in the earned sense rather than the counterfeit one. Raven names the bond correctly: not pet, not servant, but partner. The familiar chooses a place beside him, and the bond holds through clean closure.

Apprenticeship Chosen Plainly

After the successful casting Raven forces the larger question into the open: whether Edrin will truly take responsibility for teaching him, or whether Raven must seek another path. Edrin does not get to hide in indefinite caution any longer. He answers plainly that if he sent Raven away now, it would be cowardice dressed as restraint. He therefore chooses to accept Raven as his apprentice. This is one of Session 002's clearest turning points. Edrin is no longer merely the old wizard Raven keeps cornering for lessons. He is now a declared teacher with obligations, expectations, and the beginning of a real apprenticeship bond.

Homecoming — Mother, Familiar, and Household Truth

Raven returns home with the familiar active, his notes protected, and apprenticeship newly named. His mother tests the result hard but fairly. She asks not only whether the spell worked, but whether the bond is sound outside ritual space and whether Edrin judged it sound enough to let Raven leave with it. Raven preserves the integrity of his notes before waxing poetic about the bond, and that discipline wins her respect. Later, when he does describe the bond, he calls it stable and right in the earned sense of a blade that fits the hand because it has been learned, not because it arrived with false perfection. She accepts that distinction. The familiar also passes several ordinary tests in the house: it remains calm, settles beside Raven without performance, and even chooses to eat a little when food is offered, establishing that this bond belongs to daily life as much as ritual space.

The First Apprentice Morning

The next morning shows how quickly Session 002 turns magical breakthrough into ordinary life. Raven wakes with the familiar still present, begins speaking to it in quiet practical rhythm, and then turns to apprentice errands rather than fantasy. He and his mother discuss materials, incense, pouches, and the difference between bought tools and made ones. She helps him think with the same severity she applies to blades and survival: use what can be repurposed, buy only what is necessary, and test what the forest can provide rather than assuming it. This domestic practicality matters because Raven's magical life is not separating from his real life. It is being forced to live inside it.

Sword Closure and the First Salvageable Peer Bond

One of the session's most morally important secondary developments is Raven's decision to return the sword he took from the boy who tried to ambush him on the road earlier in his life. Instead of keeping it as trophy or excuse, he closes the matter cleanly by returning it to the boy's house. The confrontation becomes unexpectedly useful. The boy, Joren, is forced by his mother to admit that the ambush was not really about coin but about vulnerability, borrowed courage, and the urge to test whether Raven's confidence was real. Raven does not humiliate him further. Instead he pushes the moment toward lesson, reflection, and a future possibility: if Joren trains honestly with wooden swords, he may come spar at Raven's house someday. Session 002 therefore turns an ugly past ambush into a first narrow road toward healthier peer contact.

Practical Apprentice Work — Pouch, Incense, and the Forest Ahead

The session closes not on grandeur but on practical next steps. Raven secures an improvised component pouch at home, successfully buys a small packet of plain dried-herb incense for 2 cp at the general shop rather than overpaying at a specialist, and prepares to use that scent as a reference point for herb-gathering in the forest. This closing movement matters because it shows the true shape of apprenticeship in Raven's life: not only dramatic spellcasting, but note-taking, errands, tool judgment, and the problem of making magic fit a scarce household economy. Session 002 ends before the forest test itself, but with Raven now fully changed by what the session made real: his first familiar, his first declared apprenticeship, and the first ordinary morning after both.

Session Closing Position

Session 002 closes with Raven stronger in several distinct ways. He now has Find Familiar truly learned and successfully cast, an unnamed small dark cat-shaped spirit partner, a declared apprenticeship under Edrin, a cleaner practical path toward future spell materials, and a more stable household recognition of what his arcane life has become. He also carries 150 more XP than he began with, 2 fewer copper pieces, and a new little packet of plain dried-herb incense in his pouch. The session does not solve inherited sorcery, tears, Waterdeep, or the full future of friendship and belonging. But it does move all of them onto firmer ground. Raven ends the session still young, still poor, still watched, but no longer waiting at the edge of the life he wants to enter.
