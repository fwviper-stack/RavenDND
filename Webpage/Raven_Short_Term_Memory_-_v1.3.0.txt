Current Active Continuity

Raven is no longer merely pushing toward Find Familiar as a future lesson. He has now cast the spell successfully, closed it cleanly, and lives with an active familiar bond in ordinary household life. At the same time, Edrin is no longer a half-committed mentor but a declared teacher who has accepted Raven as his apprentice. This means Raven's current continuity has shifted from "fight to be taught" into "learn how to carry the teaching honestly."

Active Narrative Pressures

• Apprenticeship is now live under Edrin, but it immediately demands practical follow-through: bring personal materials, compare scent aids, keep notes honest, and continue learning under structure rather than triumph.

The familiar bond is active, stable, and unnamed. Its ordinary-life behavior now matters as much as the original casting scene.

Tear study remains unresolved, especially the split-boulder tear and the fact that at least one such wound is tied to Edrin's own failed working.

Possible inherited sorcery remains active and still requires testing.

Material scarcity remains active: Raven still lacks a true spellbook and must stretch coin, scavenged tools, and household improvisation into real wizardly practice.

Joren has moved from past threat into possible future sparring contact, but only if he actually grows into it honestly.

Current Emotional and Intellectual Momentum

Raven's recent continuity is no longer driven primarily by frustration at being delayed. It is driven by adjustment to success. He has crossed a threshold he wanted badly, but Session 002 repeatedly warns him not to let one good day become proof that he has become more than he is. That warning matters. Raven is excited, proud, and newly steadied, but he is also being pushed to let apprenticeship become ordinary work instead of self-myth. The familiar bond reinforces that movement: it is stable, mutual, and quiet rather than dramatic.

Active Relationship Weight

• Mother remains the strongest practical force in Raven's life. After Session 002 she no longer treats the familiar as suspect by default and now helps shape Raven's apprenticeship needs in practical terms.

Edrin now carries higher current weight because he has crossed from evasive mentor into declared teacher. His future actions matter more because he has now chosen.

The familiar has become a new active relational presence in Raven's near-current life. Its bond is defined by partnership rather than ownership.

Joren now carries low but real recent weight as a boy who may either grow into a better road or fail to do so.

Raven's father remains active in near-current relevance because Session 002 explicitly linked the fear before the casting to the danger of inherited magical self-destruction.

Recent Facts Worth Carrying Forward

• Find Familiar is no longer merely in study. Raven has learned and cast it successfully.

The familiar is currently manifested as an unnamed small dark cat-shaped spirit and is treated as Raven's partner.

Edrin explicitly accepted Raven as his apprentice before Raven left the house.

Mother accepted the familiar bond as stable enough not to default to rejection.

Raven returned Joren's borrowed sword cleanly, heard the motive behind the ambush plainly, and invited a future wooden-sword spar if Joren trains honestly.

Raven now carries a small packet of plain dried-herb incense and an improvised component pouch for future apprentice work.
