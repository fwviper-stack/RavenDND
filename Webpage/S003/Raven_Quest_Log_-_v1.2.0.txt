Quest Notes
Session 003 changes the weight of several active quests. The ring is no longer a hidden object in the background; it is now the central continuity problem. Apprenticeship remains active, but it is now tied to recovery, identification, and the discipline of surviving what Raven has already touched. The grove is not a one-scene obstacle; it is a standing environmental problem and a possible future site of investigation, revenge, or correction.
Raven's stated future research interest in a poison-and-illness cure should be remembered as a serious long-term developmental direction, even if it is not yet a formal quest of its own.
