5. Character Establishment

Session 000 establishes Raven as adaptive, observant, restrained, questioning, and resistant to easy answers. He is not yet defined by later build language.

He wants control, understanding, and a way to prevent loss. He refuses simple surrender to power and resists the idea that one path of growth must erase another.

Relationship to danger: engaged but careful.

Relationship to magic: curious, wary, and personally invested.

Relationship to family: deeply shaped by mother and father in very different ways.

6. Relationship Establishment

Raven's mother is explicit and central. She is trainer, provider of harsh safety, and the person who turns survival into daily practice.

Raven's father is absent in the present line but remains one of the most powerful forces in the archive because the loss is lived and unresolved.

Edrin is established as a cautious mentor figure rather than a free source of easy answers.

Mother - explicit: trainer, authority, pressure, survival teacher.

Father - explicit: dead through magic; unresolved source of motive and fear.

Edrin - explicit: cautious mentor; uncertain: full motives, limits, long-term role.

7. Setting and Location Establishment

The family home and daily economy matter because they keep the story grounded in labor, scarcity, and practical limits.

10. End State

Raven ends Session 000 more controlled, more aware of boundary danger, and more committed to understanding what lies behind loss and tears.
