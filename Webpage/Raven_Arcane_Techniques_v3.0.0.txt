Raven Thorne - Arcane Techniques

v3.0.0 - DOCX source update with clarified casting distinction

Core Note

Arcane Techniques use Wisdom as their casting ability. These cantrip-like techniques are rooted in Raven's early instinctive control rather than formal wizard training. This does not change the casting ability of traditional wizard spells learned later.

Arcane Techniques Casting Ability: Wisdom

Traditional Wizard Spellcasting: Unchanged; uses standard wizard casting unless a separate rule says otherwise

Theme: Innate arcane control, awareness, anchoring, and alignment rather than academic wizardry

Abjuration

Anchor Self

Type: Cantrip

Use: Bonus Action | Self | Concentration (1 min)

Effect: Advantage on saves vs charm, fear, and possession.

Limit: Raven cannot be magically tracked unless he engages.

Additional Effect: Creatures relying on awareness have disadvantage to notice him.

Boundary Set

Type: Cantrip

Use: Action | Self (5 ft aura) | Concentration (1 min)

Effect: +1 AC and advantage vs forced movement.

Additional Effect: Creatures must succeed on a Strength save to move Raven.

Aura Effect: Magical influence has disadvantage inside the aura.

Guided Center

Type: Cantrip

Use: Action | Touch | 1 min

Effect: Target gains advantage on saves vs charm, fear, and possession.

Additional Effect: Target may immediately reroll one failed save.

Willing Target Bonus: +1 bonus to all saves while the effect lasts.

Divination

Soft Awareness

Type: Cantrip

Use: Action | 30 ft | Instant

Effect: Sense presence, emotional weight, and distortions.

Limit: No exact locations are revealed.

Transmutation

Flow State

Type: Cantrip

Use: Bonus Action | Self | 1 round

Effect: +10 ft movement and advantage on Acrobatics.

Additional Effect: Opportunity attacks against Raven have disadvantage.

Hybrid (Illusion / Abjuration)

Unseen Posture

Type: Cantrip

Use: Action | Self | Concentration (1 min)

Effect: Creatures have disadvantage to notice Raven if he is not acting aggressively.

End Condition: Effect ends if Raven attacks or casts offensive magic.

Optional Level Scaling (Homebrew)

At higher levels, at DM discretion, the following expansions may apply:

Anchor Self: Extend effect to allies within 5 ft

Boundary Set: Increase aura to 10 ft

Soft Awareness: Gain vague direction of strongest presence

Flow State: Gain disengage as part of movement

Guided Center: Affect multiple allies

Unseen Posture: Partial invisibility when stationary
