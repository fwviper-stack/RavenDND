The Raven Codex — Expanded
Power without control is nothing. To act without understanding is to invite ruin.
The path does not end at twenty steps. Those who stop growing become predictable—and
predictable things break.
Death is not an end, but a transformation. But all transformations carry a cost.
The heart does not lie. The world may misunderstand you—but you must not misunderstand
yourself.
Time is not your enemy. Rushing blinds you to what is already there.
What is learned must be remembered. What is remembered becomes strength.
The journey ends only when you choose to stop walking. Until then, you adapt.
Tell your story as a whole. Fragments are for those who do not understand what they are seeing.
