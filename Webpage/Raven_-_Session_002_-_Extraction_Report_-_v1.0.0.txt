2. Start State

Raven begins Session 002 at age 12, Level 4, Fighter 2 / Wizard 2, with 5625 XP and the Session 001 end-state pressure intact. Find Familiar is at the threshold of real instruction but not yet cast. Edrin is still a conditional mentor rather than a declared master. Mother respects Raven's seriousness but continues testing whether his arcane pursuit is being taught honestly. Raven has no familiar yet, no spellbook, and no fully settled answer about inherited sorcery.

3. End State

Age 12; Level 4; Fighter 2 / Wizard 2; XP 5775 / 6500; current coin 16 cp and 12 sp on the active character sheet; Find Familiar successfully learned and cast; familiar currently manifested as an unnamed small dark cat-shaped spirit partner; improvised component pouch and small packet of plain dried-herb incense in current equipment. Narrative end-state: Edrin has explicitly accepted Raven as his apprentice, mother has accepted the familiar bond as stable enough to live in the house, and Raven has begun the first practical apprentice errands rather than merely dreaming of them.

4. Scene-by-Scene Event Chain

4.1 Morning spar and breakfast with mother: Raven frames Edrin's delay as real teaching failure if it continues indefinitely; mother pushes him to distinguish dependence from helplessness and preparation from bluff.

4.2 Waterdeep / wider-world conversation: Raven openly admits wanting a larger world and research future, while mother reframes travel and scholarship as things built through preparation, coin, and skill rather than fantasy.

4.3 Arrival at Edrin's house: Raven enters the house for the first time, confirms Edrin expected the confrontation, and pushes him into plain speech about what lesson is owed.

4.4 Find Familiar structure lesson: Edrin teaches the four-anchor model — Purpose, Invitation, Boundary, Welcome — plus materials, casting sequence, focus discipline, and failure states. Raven refines the language rather than merely repeating it.

4.5 Live casting preparation: Raven names his fear of being shaped by magic rather than shaping it rightly; Edrin accepts the fear as legitimate and non-disqualifying.

4.6 Successful casting: Raven completes the working, holds the threshold honestly, receives a small dark cat-shaped spirit answer, names the bond as partnership, and closes the spell cleanly.

4.7 Apprenticeship decision: Raven forces Edrin to answer whether he will truly teach him; Edrin accepts Raven as his apprentice and assigns real follow-up duties rather than vague hope.

4.8 Return home: mother evaluates the bond, the familiar, the notes, and Edrin's judgment. She accepts the bond as stable enough not to assume the answer is already no.

4.9 Domestic consolidation: Raven records detailed casting notes, discusses hidden peoples, social curiosity, and ordinary household life with the familiar present, and tests that this familiar can eat a little even though it does not appear to need food normally.

4.10 Next-morning spar and apprentice planning: Raven spars with mother, discusses materials, a pouch, incense, and the practical shape of his next lesson.

4.11 Sword return and reckoning: Raven returns the borrowed sword to Joren's household; Joren and his mother are forced into truth, apology, and a clearer understanding of what the roadside ambush really meant.

4.12 Possible peer repair: Raven invites Joren to someday come spar honestly with wooden swords if he actually trains and leaves steel behind.

4.13 General shop purchase: Raven buys a small packet of plain dried-herb incense for 2 cp to use as a scent reference before later forest gathering.

5. Major Revelations and Continuity Weight

Edrin's true barrier to teaching Find Familiar was his own fear of becoming the man who actually taught it, not Raven's unreadiness.

Raven's first familiar bond stabilizes around partnership, not ownership or service.

Edrin explicitly accepts Raven as his apprentice before Raven leaves the house.

Mother accepts the familiar as stable enough not to default to rejection and confirms that ordinary household life can now include the bond.

Joren admits the earlier ambush was about Raven being alone, visibly self-possessed, and worth "testing," not about meaningful coin.

Raven turns the sword return into a lesson rather than a humiliation, creating the first narrow possibility of a healthier peer relationship.

6. Emotional / Psychological Developments

Raven moves from demanding honest teaching to actually receiving it and proving he can carry it without losing discipline. This is a major internal threshold. He no longer only imagines himself as someone ready for a real spell; he now has proof.

The familiar bond confirms a version of magic Raven has been reaching toward since Session 000: structured, mutual, and honest rather than coercive or self-deluding. This helps separate inherited fear from inherited destiny.

Mother's acceptance does not become softness, but it becomes recognition. Raven now has more emotional room at home to treat arcane study as real life rather than private defiance.

Raven also shows increasing capacity to convert conflict into lesson. The Joren encounter proves he can seek truth and consequence without needing humiliation as the end-state.
